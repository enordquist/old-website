Research Interests

I'm modeling protein function by integrating experiments & biophysical simulation with statistical learning.

Hsp70 Molecular Chaperone - Substrate Affinity

I built a model of substrate affinity by training an MD-derived interactions matrix on low-resolution binding affinity data. The resulting model recovers both the binding affinity trends, but also higher resolution information in crystal structures of bound substrates. Read more (coming soon), or view the code on Github.

BK Channel Gating

I'm building a model of the BK channel to understand the effects any single mutation would have on the gating voltage and intracellular calcium concentration required for closing.

Nanopore Dewetting

In nano-scale confined environments, water exists in a delicate equilibrium between condensed and vapor phase. I am using simple model nanopores to study the effects of pore morphology and co-solvent concentration on the free energy cost of dewetting, in order to apply what I learn to real proteins like the BK channel.
